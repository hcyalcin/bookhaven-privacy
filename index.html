Privacy Policy Last updated: April 6, 2026 BookHaven does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data. Your library, reading progress, and preferences stay on your device. What stays on your device All of the following data is stored locally on your device and is never sent to any server: Your book library and imported files Reading progress, bookmarks, and highlights Reading statistics and streaks App preferences and settings Listening history and audiobook progress Cover art lookup When you import a book or browse cover art options, BookHaven may send the book title and author name to public APIs (Apple iTunes, Google Books, and Open Library) to find matching cover artwork. No personal information, device identifiers, or reading history is included in these requests. Purchases In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple through StoreKit. BookHaven does not operate its own payment server and does not receive or store your payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Apple handles transaction data. No analytics or tracking BookHaven does not use any analytics services, crash reporting tools, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies. There are no cookies, no device fingerprinting, and no behavioral tracking of any kind. No account required BookHaven does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use the app. Third-party services BookHaven uses only the following third-party services: Apple StoreKit — for processing in-app purchases (governed by Apple's privacy policy) Public book APIs — iTunes Search, Google Books, and Open Library for cover art only (book title and author sent, no personal data) Children's privacy BookHaven does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Changes to this policy If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
